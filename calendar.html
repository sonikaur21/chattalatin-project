March Social Dance

🎭💃 ChattaLatin Masquerade Social 🕺🎭

Something a little mysterious is coming to the dance floor… 👀
On March 28th, we’re turning our social into a Masquerade Night — so bring your masks, sparkles, and Mardi Gras / carnival colors and get ready for an unforgettable night of salsa & bachata. ✨
The only question is… will we even recognize each other behind the masks?
Or will you spend the night guessing who your dance partner really is? 🎭
Either way, expect a night full of great music, great dancing, and great people.
We’re also excited to welcome Krystal from Bailemos Bachata Knoxville as our guest instructor for the evening! 💃🔥

💃 Lesson: 8:00–9:00 PM

💃 Bachata Sensual Class — Step Up part I in Chattanooga 🕺

Ready to take your bachata dancing to the next level? This Bachata Sensual continuation class in Chattanooga is designed for dancers who already have their basics down and want to build more confidence, technique, and connection on the dance floor.

In this progressive 4-week bachata class, you’ll refine your movement, improve your partner connection, and learn new combinations to elevate your social dancing.

🗓 March 3, 10, 17 & 24
⏰ 9:00 PM – 10:00 PM

📍 Broad Street Ballroom
2628 Broad St
Chattanooga, TN 37411

⚠️ Prerequisite: Students must already be comfortable with bachata basics, including the basic step, timing, and simple turns.

📲 Advance registration required

Join us on our Greet and Meet events

Next meet up- March 20th at 7:00pm.

Our Greet & Meet events are a casual way to connect with the Chattanooga Latin dance community. Come hang out, enjoy good company, and make new friends before hitting the dance floor at socials. 💃🕺 It’s not a class—just a space to share laughs, talk, and build community. Everyone’s welcome, whether you dance or not! 👉 Plus, it’s the perfect ice breaker before the social the following week—you’ll already know familiar faces when you step onto the dance floor.
